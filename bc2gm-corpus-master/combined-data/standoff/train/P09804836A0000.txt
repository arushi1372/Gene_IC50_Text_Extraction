An approximately 37-kDa cytoplasmic protein is rapidly tyrosine-phosphorylated in the response of mouse BAC1.2F5 macrophages to colony stimulating factor-1 (CSF-1). pp37 was purified from the cytosolic fraction by anti-Tyr(P) affinity chromatography, size exclusion chromatography, and C4 reverse phase high pressure liquid chromatography.
