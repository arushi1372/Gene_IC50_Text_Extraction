Serum concentrations of E2 and TBOH were measured on d 0, 1, 3, 5, 7, 13, 21, 28, 42, 56, 84, 112, and 140 in finishing heifers administered the following treatments: 1) control; 2) MGA, .5 mg per heifer daily; 3) Revalor-H (140 mg TBA + 14 mg E2); 4) Revalor-H + MGA; 5) Finaplix-H (200 mg TBA); and 6) Finaplix-H + MGA.
