The effects of cardioselective beta-1 adrenergic agonist, 1-(4-hydroxyphenoxy)-isopropylamino-2-propanol), i.e., prenalterol, were examined on direct and continuous measurements of left ventricular (LV) pressure, diameter, dP/dt, velocity of shortening, arterial pressure, iliac blood flow and heart rate in 10 conscious dogs.
