Experimental study of ta chengchi tang decoction for relieving lung injury during acute necrotizing pancreatitis] The objectives of the study were to investigate the changes of leukocyte adhesiveness and tumor necrosis factor (TNF) in the early stage of acute necrotizing pancreatitis (ANP), to go further into the relation of those changes to lung injury of ANP, and to evaluate the prohibitive effect of ta chengchi tang Decoction on leukocyte adhesion and TNF secretion.
