The following markers were looked for in the serum, skin and liver of all patients with chronic hepatitis: HBV (HBs Ag and HBe Ag, anti-HBs, anti-HBe and anti-HBc in the serum, HBs and HBc Ag in the liver); non-A non-B (non-A, non-B Ag and antibody, anti non-A non-Bc in the serum, non-A, non-Bc Ag in the liver).
