The structural changes in intra-acinar artery manifested mainly by an increase in number of muscular artery resulting from muscularization of precursor cells (pericytes and intermediate cells) located within partially muscular and nonmuscular arterial wall to smooth muscle cell, and by the medial wall thickened due to hypertrophy and hyperplasia of smooth muscle cells as well as accumulation of a large amount of collagen, especially the type 1 collagen.
