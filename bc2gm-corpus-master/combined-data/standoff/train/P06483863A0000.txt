In two experiments, neonatal female BALB/cCrgl or BALB/cfC3HCrgl mice were given subcutaneous injections of 5 micrograms 17 beta-estradiol or sesame oil for the first 3 days of life and were ovariectomized at 60 days of age, at which time vaginal concretions (Experiments I and II) or silica (Experiment II) were implanted intravaginally.
