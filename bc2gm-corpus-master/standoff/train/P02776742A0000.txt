Heart rate (HR), rectal temperature (Tre), blood pressure, temperature and relative humidity changes inside clothing were measured on 18 professional firemen (mean age 29.4 +/- 7.4 yr, VO2 max 41.4 + 8.8 ml kg-1 min-1) wearing fire fighter's uniforms (SU) or aluminized, fire resistant, impermeable clothing with self contained breathing apparatus (FE).
