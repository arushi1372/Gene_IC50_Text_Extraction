The investigation was apt at studying the relationship between urinary metabolites of serotonin and catecholamine (5-HIAA and VMA), T-cells (OKT3+), T-helper (OKT4+), T-suppressor (OKT8+) and B-cells (EAC-rosette forming cells) in normal (10 subjects, 1 female, 9 male, age 21 +/- 5 years) and stress administered subjects (20 subjects, 3 female, 17 male, age 20 +/- 4 years).
