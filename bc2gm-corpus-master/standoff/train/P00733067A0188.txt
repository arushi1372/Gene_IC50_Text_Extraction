To minimize reflux into the blind loop, a number of technical steps are considered indispensable: 1) fluted section of the jejunal stump; 2) isoperistaltic construction of the anastomosis and, particularly, 3) the creation of a single or double valve system upstream from the anastomosis by spiral introflexion of the mucosa using seroserous stitches parallel and perpendicular to the ileal lumen.
