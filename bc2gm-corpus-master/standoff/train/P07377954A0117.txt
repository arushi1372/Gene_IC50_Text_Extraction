The following aspects were assessed: (1) The effect on the cardiovascular system in terms of brachial arterial pressure, pulse rate, and oxygen consumption (VO2) during exercise in 5 health volunteers; (2) emg activity of the tibialis anterior and gastrocnemius muscles during exercise in the same 5 volunteers; and (3) postexercise ankle pressure changes in 10 subjects with angiographically proven occlusive arterial diseases of the lower extremities.
