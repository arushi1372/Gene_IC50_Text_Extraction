Of the 47 women with AGCUS, 16 had intraepithelial or invasive neoplasms (34%; 95% confidence interval, 21-49%), including 9 low or high grade squamous intraepithelial lesions, 1 adenocarcinoma in situ of the cervix, 3 adenocarcinomas of the cervix, 2 adenocarcinomas of the endometrium and 1 adenoid basal cell carcinoma of the cervix.
