Animals were divided into six equal groups as follows: (1) no oral drug (water), no hemodilution, no IV drug (saline); (2) oral water, hemodilution, IV saline; (3) oral water, no hemodilution, IV propranolol; (4) oral water, hemodilution, IV propranolol; (5) oral propranolol, no hemodilution, IV saline; and (6) oral propranolol, hemodilution, IV saline.
